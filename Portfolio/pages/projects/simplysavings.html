Simply Savings

Simply Savings is an iOS-deployed budgeting app that can help users keep track of their finances and become more financially independent.

For years now, I have been writing my budgets in Python, however it wasn't very portable and it wasn't usable for the everyday man, so I set out to write a budget using SwiftUI so I can take it with me anywhere and share it with friends and family as well.

With no experience in mobile app development or swift, it was quite the experience figuring out how data is saved and loaded from the device.
