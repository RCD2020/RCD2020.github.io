Sub TTS

Encourage chatters to sub using this fun stream app that thanks your incoming subs using AI generated celebrity voices to appear on stream and thank them.

Using GPT4 to generate lines in character and then sending those lines to FakeYou, a voice generating service, and then having a character on-screen speaking those lines, you can have popular streamers, celebrities, and tv characters thank your subs!
